----------------------------------------------------------------
COURSE SUMMARY
----------------------------------------------------------------
The Pie & Tart Specialist programme is a hands-on, bakery course designed for
intermediate learners who want to gain practical, job-ready skills under the
guidance of professional chefs. Every session is conducted in a fully
equipped studio kitchen with small class sizes (max 12 students) so each
learner gets individual attention and plenty of practice.

----------------------------------------------------------------
KEY DETAILS
----------------------------------------------------------------
Duration      : 3 week(s)  (18 hours total, approx. 6 hours/week)
Course Fee    : SGD $560 (includes ingredients, apron & take-home recipes)
Location      : Sweet Heights Bakery Campus, 123 Orchard Road, #04-12, Singapore 238888
Intake Dates  : Mar, Jul, Nov
Class Size    : Maximum 12 students
Certification : Certificate in Pastry Making

----------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT YOU WILL LEARN
----------------------------------------------------------------
  - Shortcrust, sweet & puff pastry
  - Blind baking & avoiding soggy bases
  - Fruit, custard & savoury fillings
  - Lattice tops & decorative crimping

----------------------------------------------------------------
WHO SHOULD ATTEND
----------------------------------------------------------------
Home cooks and baking enthusiasts, aspiring entrepreneurs planning a home
business or cafe, and anyone looking to build a foundation for a career in
the bakery industry. No prior experience required for beginner courses.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  - Printed and digital recipe booklet
  - Branded apron to keep
  - Certificate upon successful completion
  - Take home everything you bake/cook each session
