How your application is processed

Every application runs through the same four checks, in the same order, every time. The checks stop at the first one that fails — so you always know exactly which criterion decided the outcome.

  1. 1

    Existing customer check

    We look up your NRIC or FIN in our customer records. If you already bank with us, we will not create a second record — you will be asked to visit a branch to add the new account to your existing profile.

  2. 2

    Age & identity

    Applicants must be at least 18 years old. A junior account for anyone under 18 must be opened at a branch together with a parent or legal guardian.

  3. 3

    KYC & AML screening

    As required under MAS Notice 626, we screen every applicant. If you are a Politically Exposed Person, a tax resident outside Singapore, or your funds come from a source that requires enhanced due diligence, your application is referred to our compliance team rather than declined. An officer will contact you within 5 working days.

  4. 4

    Eligibility & minimum deposit

    We check that you meet the income, employment and minimum-deposit criteria for the specific account you asked for. If you do not, we tell you which criterion was not met and suggest an account that would suit you.
